About UPolls

UPolls is a secure, modern, and user-friendly university election platform designed to empower students and administrators. Our mission is to ensure transparent, fair, and accessible elections for all.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital platform for university elections in Africa, fostering democratic values, transparency, and student engagement through technology.

Objectives

  • Promote free, fair, and credible elections in universities.
  • Empower students to participate actively in campus democracy.
  • Provide a secure, accessible, and easy-to-use voting experience.
  • Leverage technology to increase transparency and trust in the electoral process.
  • Support administrators with powerful tools for managing elections and results.
