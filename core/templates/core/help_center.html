Help Center

Having trouble logging in?

Make sure you are using your correct username and password. If you have forgotten your password, use the password reset option or contact support.

Didn't receive your 2FA code?

Check your Gmail inbox and spam folder. If you still don't see the code, wait a few minutes and try again, or contact support@upolls.com.

Issues with registration?

Ensure your matricule is valid and not already used. Only official Gmail addresses are accepted for registration.

Other questions?

Visit our FAQs or email support@upolls.com for further assistance.
