Guides

How to Register

  1. Go to the homepage and click the Register button.
  2. Fill in your details, including your official Gmail and matricule number.
  3. Check your email for a 2FA code and enter it to complete registration.

How to Vote

  1. Log in with your credentials and complete 2FA if prompted.
  2. Navigate to the Vote page from your dashboard.
  3. Review the candidates and select your preferred choices.
  4. Submit your vote. You will receive a confirmation message.

How to View Results

  1. After voting or when the election ends, go to the Results page.
  2. View the results for each position and party.
