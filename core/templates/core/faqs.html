Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote?

Click the Register button on the homepage and fill in your details, including your matricule number. You must use your official Gmail address and a valid matricule.

Can I vote more than once?

No. Each voter can only vote once per election. The system will prevent multiple votes from the same user.

How is my vote kept secure?

UPolls uses secure authentication, 2FA, and encrypted data storage to ensure your vote is confidential and tamper-proof.

When can I see the results?

You can view results immediately after you have voted, or after the election ends, depending on the election settings.

Who do I contact for support?

Please visit our Help Center or email support@upolls.com for assistance.
